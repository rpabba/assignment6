Every person is unique and complex - but you the designer need to understand them. Let us help.

The Archetype Project is made by designers for designers and our goal is to help you understand the people you are designing for. We have built comprehensive archetypes of some of the most frequently used types of people. We have collected, analyzed, and condensed data about people from all around the world to give you data that is easy to consume and actionable for all your design projects!

New Parent

New Parents

New parents are one of the hardest groups of people to convince. Their new child is the most important aspect of their lives and every decision will depend on the new child. We have an in-depth look at how you can make sure your product meets their needs and values.

Recent College Graduate

Recent college graduates are a growing population that have a specific set of goals and both socially and financially. These are key characteristics to keep in mind designing for this population. Learn what the value and how you can make sure you application supports and enhances their life.

Suburban American Family

The Suburban Family is an American staple and are most consistent group of people in to design for. The structure and family dynamic is extremely important when designing for this group - there has been many changes recently with sam sex marriages. We have analyzed not only the different types of emerging families but also how the values and beliefs has changes over time.

About Us

Hello! We are a group of HCI students at Carnegie Mellon University trying to learn how we can build better products for people. Understanding how people think, work, and make decisions is a big part of building products that work for them. We wanted to create tool for designers to be able to do just that. The Archetype Project is a design tool built by designers for designers to better understand the people they are designing for. We have collected, analyzed, and condensed data about people from all around the world to give you data that is easy to consume and actionable for all your design projects. Hope this helps you in your design journey!
